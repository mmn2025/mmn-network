PhD Scholarships

Excellence PhD Scholarship - University Paris-Saclay

Country: France

Amount: €1,500/month for 3 years

Deadline: April 15, 2025

Fields: Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics

This scholarship aims to attract the best international talents to pursue a PhD at the University of Paris-Saclay. The scholarship includes a monthly allowance and health coverage.

Fulbright Foreign Student Program

Country: United States

Amount: Full coverage

Deadline: Varies by country of origin

Fields: All fields

The Fulbright Program offers scholarships for foreign students wishing to pursue a PhD in the United States. The scholarship covers tuition, health insurance, and living expenses.

MITACS Globalink Excellence Fellowship

Country: Canada

Amount: CAD 15,000/year

Deadline: September 1, 2025

How to Apply

For each scholarship mentioned above, please follow these steps:

  1. Check your eligibility according to the specific criteria of the scholarship
  2. Prepare the required documents (typically CV, transcripts, recommendation letters, research proposal)
  3. Respect the application deadline
  4. Carefully follow the specific instructions for each scholarship program

Feel free to contact our team for advice on your scholarship application.
